### 2. **Autonomous, Not Assisted**

**Assisted AI** (Everyone else):
- "Type a prompt, get a response"
- "Copy-paste into your tools"

**Autonomous AI** (AURA OS):
- "Describe your goal, AI plans and executes"
- "Multi-step workflows across tools"
- "Self-correcting runtime"
- "Memory of your product context"

**Example**:
```

AURA OS: "Create PRD for AI search feature"
‚Üí AI researches competitors
‚Üí AI generates PRD with insights
‚Üí AI creates user stories

‚Üí AI posts in Slack
‚Üí Done in 3 minutes
```

### 3. **Memory-Native, Not Stateless**

**Stateless AI** (ChatGPT, Claude):
- Forgets everything between sessions
- You repeat context every time
- No learning from your product

**Memory-Native AI** (AURA OS):
- Remembers your product
- Knows your competitors
- Learns your preferences
- Builds on previous work

**Example**:
```
Session 1: "Create PRD for payments feature"
Session 2: "Update the payments PRD with Stripe integration"
‚Üí AURA OS remembers the PRD from Session 1
‚Üí No need to re-explain context
```

---

### 4. **End-to-End Workflows, Not Point Solutions**

**Point Solutions**:
- PRD generator (standalone)
- Research tool (standalone)
- User story writer (standalone)

**End-to-End Workflows** (AURA OS):
- Goal ‚Üí Research ‚Üí PRD ‚Üí User Stories ‚Üí Jira ‚Üí Slack
- All connected, data flows between steps
- One workflow, not 5 separate tools
